Yes, we specialize in creating custom cakes for all types of special occasions, including birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events. Our team works closely with clients to design unique cakes that reflect their vision. Whether you need a specific theme, flavor, or decoration, we ensure the final product exceeds expectations.
We offer a wide variety of cake flavors, including classic vanilla, chocolate, red velvet, coconut, and brigadeiro (a popular Vintage flavor). We also offer more exotic flavors like passion fruit, dulce de leche, and guava with cheese. If you have a specific flavor in mind, we can accommodate custom requests as well.
To ensure availability and allow us time to create the perfect cake for your event, we recommend placing your order at least 7 days in advance for standard cakes. For larger or more complex custom cakes, we recommend placing your order 2-3 weeks ahead of time. However, we also accommodate last-minute requests depending on our schedule.
Yes, we offer gluten-free and vegan cake options for customers with dietary restrictions. Our gluten-free cakes are made with alternative flours, and our vegan cakes are crafted without any animal products, ensuring everyone can enjoy a delicious treat. Be sure to mention your preferences when placing an order.
Yes, we offer delivery services within our local area. Delivery fees depend on the distance from our shop. Please inquire when placing your order to confirm if we deliver to your location and the associated cost. We also recommend scheduling your delivery time in advance to ensure prompt service on your special day.
Yes, we offer cake tastings for weddings and large events. This service allows you to sample different flavors and choose the perfect combination for your special day. Tastings are available by appointment, and we encourage you to book your tasting session as part of the planning process for your event.
Our cake prices vary depending on the size, complexity, and customization. Simple cakes start at an affordable rate, while more elaborate designs and larger cakes will be priced accordingly. We provide a detailed quote once we have the specifics of your order. Feel free to contact us for a consultation and pricing based on your needs.

Whether you're looking for a simple, elegant design or a vibrant, themed cake based on a popular character or trend, our decorators can bring your ideas to life.
